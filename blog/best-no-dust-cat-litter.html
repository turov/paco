Best no dust cat litter

If you own a cat in your house, then you certainly came across the unpleasant smell of a litter tray. Since cats moved to city apartments, the question arose of how to deal with their toilet needs. Unfortunately, most of the feline friends flatly refuses to walk on a leash, like dogs and prefers not to leave the confines four walls of the apartment under any circumstances.

All cats are extremely clean, so they consider it necessary to do their business in the same place, so accustoming them to a specially equipped toilet is usually quite simple. However, the cats have one feature: they are used to thoroughly bury their feces - so in the wild, these predators hide their presence from potential victims to facilitate their hunt.

Fortunately, in our time the problems of the cat toilet have long been solved due to special litters, which are not only able to perfectly absorb moisture, but also absorb unpleasant odors. Now, it is not necessary to change the contents of the tray whenever the cat does its business, and in some cases, the litter can even simply be flushed down the toilet and not thrown into the garbage.

And today, there are so many types of cat litter on sale that you sometimes wonder: how to choose best cat litter without dust?
