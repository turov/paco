What is organic kitty litter?

Organic kitty litter is a type of cat litter made from natural and eco-friendly materials such as wood, paper, corn, wheat, and recycled materials.

You ask what is the use of it? The main function is the ability to decompose naturally under the influence of bacteria and microorganisms. With this type of organic kitty litter, we solve the litter problem and ensure the efficient disposal of used fillers.
