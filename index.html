What is organic cat litter for kittens?

Organic kitty litter is a litter that is often made from materials that are biodegradable and environmentally friendly, such as wood, paper, corn, or wheat.

In this way, the use of organic litter can prevent possible allergic reactions or irritation in the kittens, and thanks to the ability to naturally decompose under the influence of bacteria and microorganisms, this kitty litter is good for the environment.

2) COMBINE WITH EXISTING LITTER

While you're in the beginning stages, we suggest mixing one part Paco and Pepper Organic Cat Litter with two parts current litter. What is it for? In this way, you gradually introduce the product for your feline companion to get used to the new texture and smell.

3) WHEN YOUR FELINE COMPANION IS COMFORTABLE

And now you need to carefully watch your cat. What is important for you is the behavior and level of comfort of your cat.
